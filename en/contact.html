Directions

 

From Paris or Lyon

Take the highway A7 to Valence and Marseille Exit Carpentras Sud and follow the D950 to Capentras Centre In Carpentras Cross the city and follow direction Mazan and than Sault D942. When you arrive in Villes sur Auzon take the road D1 straight ahead via Le Col des Abeilles to Sault (not the touristical route). In Sault you have to cross the bridge on the right D943 to Saint Jean . After 6 km you will arrive in Saint Jean and at the pedestrian crossing take right and first left . Mon Ventoux " Villa La Lorraine " is located at 350 m at the left side.



From Marseille

Take the highway A7 to Lyon and exit Avignon Nord ( Le pontet) Take direction Carpentras . In Carpentras Cross the city and follow direction Mazan and than Sault D942 . When you arrive in Villes sur Auzon take the road D1 straight ahead via Le Col des Abeilles to Sault (not the touristical route). In Sault you have to cross the bridge on the right D943 to Saint Jean . After 6 km you will arrive in Saint Jean and at the pedestrian crossing take right and first left . Mon Ventoux " Villa La Lorraine " is located at 350 m at the left side.
