Room Prices

2 nights or more (2 people) €95/night
1 night (2 people) €110/night
2 nights or more (4 people : Lilas + Olives) €170/night
1 night (4 people : Lilas + Olives) €200/night
 

Reservations & information

  • Price per night, breakfast included.
  • Reservations are effective upon receipt of a deposit equal to 30% of the total stay. In case of cancellation, the deposit will be retained by the proprietors.
  • The remainder is to be paid 1 month before arrival.
    If the balance is not paid on time, Mon Ventoux  has the right to rent the room  to a third party.
  • In case of an interrupted stay, the total reservation must still be paid.
  • Neither cheques, or credit cards are accepted.
  • For reasons of hygiene, our animal friends are not accepted.
  • The house is non-smoking, but ashtrays are at your disposal on the terraces.
  • We speak English, French, Dutch and a little bit of German.

Arrivals & Departures

  • Arrivals: We welcome you every day from 4 to 6 pm (please notify us for a later arrival).
  • Departures: The rooms are to be cleared at the latest by 10:30 am on departure day.
