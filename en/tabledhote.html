The table d'hôtes

Myriam graduated in catering and specialised in gastronomic cooking for 4 years. Her passion for cooking with quality products is the basis of her kitchen at Mon Ventoux.

Breakfast

Breakfast is served between 8.30u and 10.00u am, on the terrace if it’s beautiful weather or in the dining room.

  • Freshly squeezed orange juice 
  • Fresh bread and croissants delivered by the local bakery
  • Jams & honey from the farm
  • Boiled eggs
  • Ham and cheese
  • Fresh fruit
  • Yogurt
  • Cereals
  • Tea, coffee, or hot chocolate

Table d’hôtes menu

All dishes are home made. The table d’hôtes is served every day except Wednesday and Sunday. A reservation is necessary with a 48h advance notice. Vegetarian dish possible on request.

  • An aperitif and appetisers
  • A starter
  • The main dish
  • A dessert
